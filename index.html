About

I'm Vedanta S P, a builder and researcher focused on turning research into deployable AI systems. I'm currently a Software Engineer at Parallel Distribution, where I build AI video-generation pipelines and develop full-stack automation suites for content repurposing workflows.

My research interests lie at the intersection of AI alignment, multi-agent systems, and responsible AI deployment. I've collaborated with Meta Research on discrete diffusion models for multi-step reasoning and worked as an NLP Research Intern at IIIT Hyderabad (Precog), focusing on reducing demographic bias in language models and building interpretability tools for model analysis.

Some projects I'm proud of include LipSync++ (neural lip-sync with GFPGAN + Stable Diffusion), dRAGarys (a retrieval-augmented generation engine handling 1M+ docs with 150ms latency), and a 360° Feedback Platform that processes multilingual feedback with 95% sentiment accuracy.

I'm pursuing my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam. My recent work explores how autonomous agents develop emergent behaviors in multi-agent environments—findings I share through these blogs.

These blogs document my research findings, experiments, and explorations in AI alignment, agent behavior, and the emergent properties of artificial intelligence systems.
